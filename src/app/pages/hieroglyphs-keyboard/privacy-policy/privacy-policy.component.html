Hieroglyphs Keyboard Privacy Policy

The Bastet-Team has developed the Hieroglyphs Keyboard app. This Privacy Policy explains how we protect your privacy when you use our free Android app, Hieroglyphs Keyboard ("the App"), which provides a keyboard for typing Hieroglyphics.

1. No Data Collection

Our app is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Connect to the internet
  • Store any data
  • Share any information with third parties

Everything you type stays on your device and is not sent anywhere.

2. Safe for Everyone

Our app is:

  • Safe for children of all ages
  • Free from advertisements
  • Free from in-app purchases
  • Free from any tracking

3. How the App Works

The Hieroglyphs Keyboard works completely offline:

  • All keyboard features work without internet connection
  • No data is sent to any servers
  • No updates are downloaded automatically
  • Your typing remains private on your device

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
